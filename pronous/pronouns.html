nouns

A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

Kinds of noun

Nouns are classified as follows:

Proper: It is the name of
Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions or actions.

pronouns

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.

Kinds of pronouns

Pronuons are classified as follow:

Personal Pronouns: It refers to a specific noun and used as a subtitute of noun.

Interrogative pronouns: It stand as the answer to the quetion about person, place or a thing.

Indefinite prnouns: It is used to refer to a person, things or a place that are unknown.

Relative prnouns: it relaes to a noun, pronoun, word or express preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.

Demonstrative pronouns: a noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it..
